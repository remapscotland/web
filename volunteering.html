REMAP Scotland relies entirely on volunteers with the collective skills and creativity necessary to come up with original solutions.

The work is sometimes challenging, but always rewarding: At present we have14 area panels covering most of Scotland (see the Panel Members Map for details of locations).

All panels welcome additional members who can help with projects and provide administrative, marketing or development support.

Can you help?

If you have a skill which you think would help then please get in touch with your local panel. It may be, for example:


engineering
occupational therapy
physiotherapy
public relations
photography
administration
assistive technology
computing
craftwork
electrics & electronics
product design

Further Information

For more details please email remap-scotland@btconnect.com or visit the the Panel Members Map on this website to get in touch with your local panel directly.

We look forward to hearing from you.
